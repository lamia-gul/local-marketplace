Returns & Refunds

Your satisfaction is our priority

7-Day Return Policy

Not satisfied? Return your items within 7 days for a full refund

How to Return an Item

1

Contact the Seller

Log in to your account and initiate a return request from your order history.

2

Pack the Item

Ensure the item is in its original condition with all tags and packaging intact.

3

Ship It Back

Use the prepaid return label provided or arrange pickup through our courier partner.

4

Get Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging and tags must be intact
  • Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation) required
  • Perishable items (food, flowers) cannot be returned
  • Custom or personalized items are non-returnable

Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued to your original payment method. If you paid by:

  • Credit/Debit Card: Refund will appear in 5-7 business days
  • Mobile Wallet: Instant refund to your wallet
  • Cash on Delivery: Bank transfer within 7-10 business days
