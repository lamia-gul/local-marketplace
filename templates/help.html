Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a store on Local Marketplace?

Click on "Create Store" in the navigation menu, fill out the registration form with your business details, and submit. Our team will review your application within 24 hours.

What are the fees for selling on Local Marketplace?

Local Marketplace charges a small commission on each sale. There are no upfront fees or monthly subscriptions. You only pay when you make a sale.

How do I track my orders?

Log in to your account and go to "My Orders" to see all your order history and tracking information. You'll also receive email updates for each order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, and cash on delivery for eligible orders.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location and store. Most local orders are delivered within 24-48 hours. You'll see estimated delivery times at checkout.
